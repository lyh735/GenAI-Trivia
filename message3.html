AI Doesn’t Understand Time the Way You Do

When the AI was asked if it could help finish three assignments in under an hour, it said yes. But AI doesn’t feel time pressure or understand real-world urgency.

It can overestimate what’s possible and give optimistic answers — even when the deadline is unrealistic.

AI can help with ideas, but it can’t manage time for you. Always apply your own judgment, especially when deadlines are involved.
